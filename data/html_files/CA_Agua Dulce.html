OurTrees Story

The impacts of tree benefits can be hard to grasp. Below are some real-world examples of how trees work hard for our community.

Trees in Agua Dulce, CA

Trees lower air temperature and absorb water, while impervious areas do the opposite.
Trees shade an area equivalent to 446 professional football fields!
The land area covered by impervious surfaces typically buildings and pavement is like a 0.7 square mile parking lot.

Annual Tree Benefits for
Agua Dulce, CA

Room for Trees:

  • Existing canopy: 590 acres

A 10% increase in canopy area, 590 acres to 649 acres, would add $557 in tree benefits to your community.

This increase would include the storage of an additional 1,991 tons of carbon, 39,230 more gallons of avoided storm water runoff, and the removal of an extra 7,896 pounds of air pollution.
